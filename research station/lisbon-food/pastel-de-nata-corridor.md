# Best Pastel de Nata — Chiado to Aljube Corridor

Research on the best pastel de nata along the walking route from Taberna da Rua das Flores (Chiado) to Museu do Aljube (near the Sé Cathedral).

## The winner: Pastelaria Santo António

**Won first place at the 11th National Contest of Pastel de Nata in 2019** — a legitimate blind competition judged by pastry professionals, not a reader poll.

- Opened 2017 by a single family. No chain, no marketing machine.
- **Distinctive lemon note** in the custard — the signature differentiator.
- €1.20/pastel (cheaper than Manteigaria or Castro).

### Two locations — one at each END of the walking corridor:
- **Chiado branch (opened 2025)**: Rua Paiva de Andrada 8, near Largo do Chiado. ~4 min from Taberna da Rua das Flores.
- **Original location**: Rua Milagre de Santo António 10, Alfama/Castelo. **3-5 min walk from Museu do Aljube.** Blue-and-white tiled storefront.

## Ranked options on the corridor

### 🥇 Pastelaria Santo António — the national award
- 2019 National Contest winner. Distinct lemon profile. Small family operation. €1.20.
- Branches at both ends of the walk.

### 🥈 Castro — Atelier de Pastéis de Nata — the refined pick
- **Rua Garrett 38** (on the route) + Rua Áurea 254 (Baixa) + Praça da Figueira 3C
- Every day 08:30-22:00
- Custard **aged 24 hours before baking** — more complex, less sweet, more egg-forward than Manteigaria.
- Launched end of 2021. The connoisseur's pick.

### 🥉 Manteigaria Chiado — the crowd-pleaser
- **Rua do Loreto 2**, 2 min from Taberna. Open 8am-midnight.
- Sweeter, creamier, super flaky crust, **deliberately scorched/caramelized top** (bittersweet caramel note).
- Most-visited non-Belém pastel de nata in Lisbon. Watch them make tarts through glass.
- "The most famous but not the technical best."

## The cleanest summary
> "Belém is history, Manteigaria is theater, Castro is refinement." Santo António is the award.

## The recommended play
Eat one **Castro** at the start (Rua Garrett 38), eat one **Santo António** at the end (near the Aljube). Compare. €2.80 for both.
